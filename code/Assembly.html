PC Assembly Manual

A step-by-step guide to building your system safely and efficiently.

  1. 1. Prepare Workspace

    Clear a large, non-conductive surface. Gather your tools and ensure you are grounded to prevent static discharge.

  2. 2. Core Component Install

    Install the CPU and RAM onto the motherboard before placing it in the case. Align the notches carefully to avoid pin damage.

    [Image of installing CPU into motherboard socket]
  3. 3. Motherboard Seating

    Install the I/O shield, then lower the motherboard onto the case standoffs. Secure it with screws in a star pattern.

  4. 4. Power Supply (PSU)

    Mount the PSU in its bay. Ensure the intake fan is positioned to draw in cool air from outside the case.

  5. 5. Cable Management

    Route the 24-pin and 8-pin power cables. Connect the front panel headers for the power button and USB ports.

  6. 6. Graphics Card (GPU)

    Slot the GPU into the primary PCIe lane. Connect the required PCIe power cables from the PSU until they click.

    [Image of installing GPU into PCIe slot]
